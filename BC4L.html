Our Book Club

Four friends. One book at a time.

What started as a way to stay connected after college has turned into something we genuinely love. Every few months, we pick a book, read it on our own, and come together to talk about it — sometimes agreeing, sometimes arguing, always having a good time. This site is our little corner of the internet to track what we've read, celebrate our picks, and remember why we started.
